ReDD Focus

How-to Guide

Hide distractions on any website

1

Turn on extension

Open Safari, go to youtube.com (or any site), and enable ReDD Focus.

↓
↓
i

Can't turn it on? You likely have Screen Time restrictions running. Turn them off in Settings → Screen Time and try again.

2

Grant permissions

Allow ReDD Focus to modify websites.

↓
OPTIONAL

Create home screen shortcuts

⌃

Open apps distraction-free in Safari with one tap.

Example for Instagram:

  1. Open the Shortcuts app and tap + to create a new shortcut
  2. Tap the name of your new shortcut (in the top) and rename it to e.g., "Instagram Safari"
  3. Search for the "Open URLs" action and tap it
  4. Tap URL and input "instagram.com"
  5. Tap the 'Share' icon in the bottom right and select 'Add to Home Screen'
  6. Select the icon you want and tap 'Add'
